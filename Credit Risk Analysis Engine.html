Disclaimer: This model is designed for educational and portfolio demonstration purposes. It simulates commercial credit underwriting methodology but does not constitute actual financial, legal, or lending advice. Real-world underwriting incorporates credit bureau data, collateral appraisals, industry-specific covenant packages, regulatory capital requirements, and institution-specific credit policy. DSCR calculations assume an estimated new loan payment based on the requested loan amount at a 6.5% interest rate over a 5-year amortization schedule. All ratios are point-in-time and should be evaluated alongside multi-year trend analysis in a real underwriting context.
